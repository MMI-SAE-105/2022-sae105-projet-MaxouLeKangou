Steven Spielberg of his real name, Steven Allan Spielberg was born on December 18 in Ohio. He made his debut in the Universal studio. His first big success was a TV movie called Duel. Then, he continues to direct, produce and win many awards. Spielberg is now one of the pioneers of cinema, he is even considered the boss of Hollywood.
