About

Capsul is a project realized by Maxime LEGRAND. The goal of his project is to make a kind of "time capsule". To save some things of this time, in order to rediscover them in a more or less near future.

Place

Paris is the capital of France. An architect named Vincent Callebaut imagines the new Paris of 2050 with vegetal towers.

Book

The Cartographers is a novel by S.E. GROVE. It is a fantasy story that brings together cartography and adventure in a world turned upside down.

Music

Back To You is a song by Selena Gomez. It is part of the original soundtrack of the series 13 Reason Why

Movie

Ready Player One is a film directed by Steven Spielberg. It is an adaptation of the novel Player One by Ernest Cline.

Regret

The world is still in overconsumption. Recycling is only partial and waste is left to be abandoned without any other life.

Hope

The world is trying to wake up. Global warming is frightening and we are now trying to limit it by creating green spaces.
